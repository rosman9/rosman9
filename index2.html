I am a post-doctoral associate at MIT/CSAIL with Daniela Rus and John W. Fisher III, with whom I work on inference and understanding of 3D and visual data from robots and mobile/wearable devices.

I've done my PhD at the department of Computer Science at the Technion, under the supervision of Ron Kimmel. My dissertation is available here

My main academic interests are:

  • Modeling and understanding in visual and 3D sensing and robotic perception.
  • Variational methods and Optimization techniques, efficient regularization and segmentation for images, 3D data, and motion.
  • Large-scale visual summaries for computer vision.
  • Nonlinear dimensionality reduction and Manifold learning.

Companies I worked in/consulted include IBM's Haifa Research Labs, part time ( including a paper), Medicvision, and Invision Biometrics (acquired by Intel).
