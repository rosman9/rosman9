Driving Dataset

This dataset contains 170 hours of driving videos used as part of our IROS'17 submission

G. Rosman, L. Paull, D. Rus, Hybrid Control and Learning with Coresets for Autonomous Vehicles, IROS 2017.

Data was collected via GoPro 4 cameras on a Toyota RAV4, installed at the top part of the driver's windshield, and recording HD video with the audio stream removed. Data was collected in area spanning 25km in the greater Boston area.
